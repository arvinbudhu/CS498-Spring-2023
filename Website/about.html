Meet the Owner

Meet Yogeeta Singh, the proud owner of this jewelry store. Born and raised in Georgetown, Guyana, Yogeeta moved to New York to pursue her dreams. After working multiple jobs, she finally found her calling in the jewelry industry and decided to buy a store with her brother, Rudradat. Together, they work tirelessly to provide their customers with the highest quality jewelry and exceptional service. While Rudradat is the talented jeweler behind the designs, Yogeeta manages the sales and even runs her own beauty supply section of the store. With her passion for the industry and unwavering dedication to her business, Yogeeta and Rudradat are both committed to providing their customers with the best possible experience.
